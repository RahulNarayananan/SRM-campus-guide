ABOUT

Welcome to SRM Campus Guide, the free interactive website that provides you with all the information you need to navigate around the SRM campus.
Our website is designed to help you make the most out of your time on campus.
Whether you are a new student, a faculty member, or a visitor, we are here to guide you through every step of your journey.
We have worked hard to create a comprehensive database of all the buildings, facilities, and amenities available on campus.
With our easy-to-use map, you can quickly find your way to your destination, and our detailed descriptions will give you a clear understanding of what to expect when you get there.
At SRM Campus Guide, we believe that knowledge is power. That's why we are committed to providing accurate and up-to-date information about the campus.
Our website is constantly updated with the latest news, events, and developments, so you can stay informed and connected with the SRM community.
Thank you for choosing SRM Campus Guide as your go-to resource for navigating the campus. We hope you find our website helpful and informative, and we look forward to helping you make the most of your time at SRM.
